Ecuador is a small country located in South America, Its capital city is Quito, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its well-preserved colonial architecture. The country is famous for its incredible geographical diversity, which includes the Amazon rainforest, the Andes mountains, and the Galápagos Islands—a unique archipelago renowned for its role in Charles Darwin’s theory of evolution. Also one of the few countries located on the equator, from which it derives its name.
