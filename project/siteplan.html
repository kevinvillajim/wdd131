Technology eCommerce Shop - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: TechBuy

Description: A specialized online store focusing on selling technology products with a user-friendly and efficient design.

Optional domain availability: techbuy.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a platform to showcase and sell technology products. It will include a homepage with featured products, a category view, and a product detail page.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the best deals on laptops and accessories?
  • How do I compare different smartphone specifications before purchasing?

Color Schema

Primary Color: rgb(48, 85, 55) (Used for headings, buttons, and accents)

Secondary Color: rgb(216, 216, 216) (Used for background and contrast)

Typography

Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif

Body Text: 'Arial', sans-serif
